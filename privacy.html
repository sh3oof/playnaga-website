Section 01

Overview

Welcome to NAGA ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by playnaga.live. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you download and play our mobile application on iOS and Android devices.

By downloading or using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

🐍 Short version: We collect only what's necessary to run the game and improve your experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Username or display name you choose within the Game
  • Email address, if you register an account or sign up for launch notifications
  • Customer support messages or feedback you submit to us

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and OS version
  • Unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID / IDFA / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Gameplay data: scores, session length, game modes played, level progress
  • In-app purchase history and transaction identifiers (not full payment card data)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Date and time of access, features used

Information from Third Parties

  • If you sign in via Apple ID or Google Play Games, we receive a unique identifier and display name from that service
  • Advertising networks may share aggregated, anonymized data about ad interactions

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Game and its features
  • Provide and manage your account, progress, and in-game purchases
  • Process and fulfill in-app purchases through Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Enable real-time multiplayer matchmaking and leaderboard features
  • Personalize your in-game experience (e.g., Serpent DNA system)
  • Send you service-related notifications (e.g., game updates, maintenance)
  • Send you marketing communications if you have opted in (you may opt out at any time)
  • Respond to your customer support inquiries
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and cheating
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage trends to improve game design and user experience

Section 04

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the Game (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, customer support tools). These parties are bound by data processing agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Multiplayer Features: Your username and rank may be visible to other players in leaderboards, match lobbies, and public rankings.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before this occurs.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit prior consent.

Section 05

Advertising & Analytics

NAGA may display optional rewarded advertisements (e.g., watch an ad to earn bonus in-game coins). We use third-party advertising and analytics services including:

  • Unity Ads — rewarded video advertising
  • Google AdMob / AppLovin MAX — ad mediation and delivery
  • Firebase Analytics / GameAnalytics — gameplay analytics and performance tracking

These services may collect your advertising ID, device information, and interaction data to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

Purchasing the "Remove Ads" option disables all interstitial ads. Optional rewarded ads remain available but are never mandatory.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

NAGA is rated for ages 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@playnaga.live. We will promptly delete such information from our records.

If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use the Game or provide any information about yourself to us.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained while your account remains active
  • Gameplay data and progress is retained to provide continuity of service
  • Upon account deletion request, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot identify you
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data are retained for a maximum of 90 days

To request deletion of your account and associated data, email us at support@playnaga.live with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Restriction: Request restriction of how we use your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@playnaga.live. We will respond within 30 days. These rights apply to users in the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA), and other applicable jurisdictions.

Section 09

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.
